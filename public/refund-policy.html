Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

CloudHop is a free gaming and entertainment platform. This refund policy applies to any optional paid services or premium features.

1. Free Services

The majority of CloudHop services are provided free of charge. No refunds are applicable to free services.

2. Premium Features (If Applicable)

If CloudHop offers premium or paid features in the future:

2.1 Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase if:

  • You request a refund before using the premium feature
  • The service was not delivered as described
  • A technical issue prevented you from accessing the service

2.2 Refund Ineligibility

Refunds are not available for:

  • Services fully used or consumed
  • Changes to features (when reasonable notice is provided)
  • User error or preference changes
  • Services used beyond the 14-day refund period

2.3 Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Email refunds@cloudhop.cloud with:
    • Your account email
    • Order date and amount
    • Reason for refund request
  2. We will respond within 5 business days
  3. Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days

3. Automatic Billing

If you have an active subscription:

  • Billing occurs on the renewal date specified
  • You will receive a reminder before renewal
  • To cancel, log in to your account settings or contact support

4. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Through your account settings
  • By emailing support@cloudhop.cloud
  • Cancellation is effective immediately
  • No refunds for partially used billing periods

5. In-Game Purchases

Some games on CloudHop may offer in-game purchases. These are typically:

  • Controlled by third-party game developers
  • Subject to their respective refund policies
  • Not covered by this CloudHop Refund Policy

6. Chargebacks

Disputing a charge through your bank ("chargeback") without first attempting resolution with us:

  • May result in account termination
  • May result in legal action
  • May affect your ability to use CloudHop services

Please contact us first to resolve billing disputes.

7. Contact Us

For refund questions or disputes:

  • Email: refunds@cloudhop.cloud
  • Response time: 5 business days
  • Website: https://cloudhop.cloud
