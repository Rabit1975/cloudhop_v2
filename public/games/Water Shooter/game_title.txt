DESCRIPTION
Welcome to the 'Water Shooter'! In this exciting first-person shooter game, you will find yourself in the role of a young hero who received a water pistol as a gift. Armed with this reliable weapon, you boldly go to the dungeon, where battles with fiery monsters await you. Immerse yourself in a fascinating world of adventures, explore mysterious corners and show everyone that your water trunk has the power of victory over fire!

INSTRUCTIONS
Management: WASD - movement. Mouse movement - aiming. Left mouse button - fire (i.e. water :)) Esc or Tab - pause/opening the menu Space - jump. To start a level, click the "Levels" button in the main menu, then select a level from the list and press "Start" to start it. Fight in the arena, score more points than other players by selecting the "Arena" item in the main menu.
