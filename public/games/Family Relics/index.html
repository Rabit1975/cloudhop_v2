About this game:
Welcome to the Parsley estate, situated in the beautiful and secluded Willow Hills! Passed down by your estranged uncle, it's all yours now. Clean and build up your farm, grow crops, and take care of the animals. However, don't be fooled by the rural farm life, because life in Willow Hills is anything but ordinary: mysterious events occur, and there are whispers about treasured relics of the Parsley family. Step into this curious adventure today!
