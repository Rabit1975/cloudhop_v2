DESCRIPTION
99 Balls 3D is an endless arcade game that mixes brick breaker and bubble shooter action in full 3D. Launch balls to smash numbered cylinders before they reach the bottom. Collect stars to unlock fun ball skins like sports balls, gems, or fruit. Simple to play, endlessly challenging, and highly addictive!

INSTRUCTIONS
Aim by dragging backward, then release to fire balls. Each cylinder needs hits equal to its number. Clear as many as possible before any reach the bottom line. Use the aiming guide to plan bounces, shoot through gaps for better angles, and fast-forward when the screen fills up. PC: click & drag mouse. Mobile: tap & drag, then release.
